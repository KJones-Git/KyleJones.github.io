About Me

Before pursuing a career in data analytics, I spent several years working at my family’s restaurant, where I developed a strong work ethic, creative problem-solving skills, and a deep understanding of consumer behavior. Managing daily operations and navigating the unpredictability of the restaurant industry taught me to adapt quickly, think on my feet, and always put the customer at the center. It also sparked my appreciation for storytelling and how branding, customer experience, and data all intersect to create lasting impressions. This curiosity led me to earn a degree in Business Data Analytics and transition into a field where I could combine real-world experience with analytical expertise. Today, I view data not just as numbers but as a powerful tool for uncovering insights and telling meaningful stories. If you’d like to connect or learn more about my work, feel free to reach out!
